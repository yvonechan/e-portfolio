Development Kick-Start Meeting

Date: 24 July 2022 

Discussed 

  1. Team dynamics: 
    1. Keep frequent communication via Slack 
    2. If any barrier is hit, voice out as soon as possible to seek help 
    3. Help each other as much as possible as each of us only works part time and have other engagements 
  2. Development: 
    1. Use GitHub as repository 
    2. When coding for a story, please also document the testing method 
  3. Scrum process: 
    1. Sprint length of 1 week 
    2. Definition of done: Another team member to test the function/ demonstration of working code 
    3. Hold sprint review and retrospective each Sunday 13:00 GMT 
    4. Sprint planning and story prioritization done after the sprint review 
    5. Stories will be re-prioirzted based on the capacity of the team and urgency of the task
