Secure Software Development (Computer Science)

Learning Outcomes:

  1. Identify & manage security risks as part of a software development project
  2. Critically analyse development problems and determine appropriate methodologies, tools and techniques to solve them
  3. Design, develop and adapt programs and to produce a solution that meets the design brief and critically evaluate solutions that are produced
  4. Systematically develop and implement the skills required to be effective member of a development team in a virtual professional environment, adopting real-life perspectives on team roles and organisation
